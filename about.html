About Agrominch Crop Care Pvt Ltd

Pioneering Nutritional Solutions for Plants and Animals

Agrominch Crop Care Private Limited is a leader in the field of
micronutrients and nutritional products, committed to advancing
the agricultural and livestock sectors. Established with a focus on
creating mineral feed additives for animals and birds, we have
grown to offer a diverse range of products, including specialized
mineral additives for agricultural use. Our journey reflects our
dedication to innovation, quality, and sustainability in everything we
do.

Our Mission


To empower farmers and livestock owners with high-quality,
customized nutritional solutions that promote growth, health, and
sustainability.



Our Vision


To be at the forefront of agricultural and livestock nutrition,
driving innovation and setting new standards for quality and
effectiveness in the industry.
